I'm a Technical Program Manager, author, and speaker with over a decade of
experience leading complex, cross-functional programs at scale. My work sits at
the intersection of engineering, product, and people — helping teams ship the
right things, on time, without burning out.

I started my career as a software engineer before moving into program and team
leadership roles, where I discovered that the hardest problems in tech are rarely
technical — they're about communication, prioritization, and trust. That shift
shaped everything I do now, from the programs I run to the books I write and the
people I coach.

## What I focus on

Cross-functional program leadership, stakeholder communication, and helping
engineers and PMs navigate career transitions — whether that's landing a TPM
role, preparing for a promotion, or building influence without formal authority.

Outside of my day job, I write books and host podcasts on careers and leadership
in tech, and coach professionals 1:1 through resume reviews, mock interviews, and
career strategy sessions. I'm based in Bengaluru, India.
