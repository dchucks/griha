A strong resume is your first impression — and most resumes fail in the first ten seconds.

In this session, we'll go through your resume line by line, covering:

- Structure and formatting for readability and ATS systems
- Impact-driven bullet points (quantified results, not just responsibilities)
- Tailoring your resume for specific roles (TPM, PM, Engineering Manager, etc.)
- Common mistakes that get resumes rejected before a human even reads them

You'll leave with a clear action plan and a markup of exactly what to change.
