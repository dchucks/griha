TPM interviews test a unique blend of technical depth, program judgment, and communication.

This mock interview is modeled on real loops at major tech companies and covers:

- Program/project sense questions (prioritization, risk management, tradeoffs)
- Technical depth questions appropriate to your background
- Behavioral and leadership-without-authority scenarios
- Communication style and structuring your answers (e.g., STAR, frameworks)

You'll receive honest, actionable feedback — the kind that's hard to get from friends or family.
