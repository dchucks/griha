This course distills years of on-the-job learning into a structured, six-module
curriculum for anyone starting out — or transitioning into — technical program
management.

## Modules

1. The role of a TPM: scope, expectations, and stakeholders
2. Roadmapping and prioritization frameworks
3. Risk identification and mitigation
4. Running effective status updates and reviews
5. Influencing without authority
6. Tools and rituals that scale with team size

## Format

Each module includes short video lessons, downloadable templates, and a practice
scenario based on real (anonymized) program situations.

> The goal isn't to memorize frameworks — it's to build judgment you can apply
> under pressure.

New cohorts open periodically with live Q&A sessions.
