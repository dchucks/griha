This template helps you turn vague career goals ("get promoted," "switch roles")
into a concrete plan with checkpoints, owners, and evidence.

## Sections

- Current state assessment (skills, scope, visibility)
- Target role definition and gap analysis
- Quarter-by-quarter milestones
- Evidence log (projects, feedback, metrics)
- Manager conversation prompts

## Who it's for

Anyone preparing for a promotion case, a role transition, or simply wanting more
intentionality around their next 12 months at work.

> Most people don't fail to get promoted because they aren't good enough — they
> fail because no one (including them) tracked the evidence.

sessions.
