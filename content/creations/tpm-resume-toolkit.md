The TPM Resume Toolkit is a set of editable templates, bullet-point formulas, and
before/after examples designed specifically for technical program managers,
project managers, and engineering leaders moving into PM roles.

## What's inside

- Three resume templates (ATS-safe, modern, and executive formats)
- A library of 80+ impact-driven bullet point examples
- A cover letter template with fill-in-the-blank prompts
- A one-page "resume audit" checklist

## Why I built it

After reviewing hundreds of resumes through 1:1 sessions, I kept seeing the same
gaps — generic bullet points, poor structure, and missing quantification. This
toolkit packages those fixes into something anyone can use on their own.

> A resume isn't a biography. It's a sales document for a role you haven't gotten yet.

The toolkit is updated periodically based on feedback from real interview outcomes.
