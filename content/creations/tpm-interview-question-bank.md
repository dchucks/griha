A categorized collection of interview questions gathered from real TPM, PM, and
engineering leadership loops — paired with frameworks and example answer outlines.

## Categories covered

- Program sense and prioritization
- Conflict resolution and stakeholder management
- Technical depth and architecture discussions
- Behavioral and leadership scenarios
- Estimation and tradeoff questions

## How to use it

Each question includes a short note on what interviewers are typically evaluating,
plus a suggested structure (not a script) for your answer.

> Preparation isn't about having the perfect answer memorized — it's about
> recognizing the pattern fast enough to think clearly under pressure.

Pairs well with the [TPM Mock Interview](/services/tpm-mock-interview/) service for
live practice.
