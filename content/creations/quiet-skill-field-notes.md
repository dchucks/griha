A companion workbook to *The Quiet Skill: Influence Without Authority*, designed
to turn the book's ideas into practiced habits.

## What's included

- Chapter-by-chapter reflection prompts
- Real-world scenario exercises
- A self-assessment for identifying your influence style
- A 30-day practice tracker

## Why a companion guide

Books are good at changing how you think. Workbooks are better at changing how you
act. This guide bridges that gap with short, low-effort exercises you can fit
into a normal workweek.

> Influence is built in small moments, repeated consistently — not in one big
> speech.

Available as a free download for readers of the book.
